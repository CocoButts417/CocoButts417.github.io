Career Planning

At CoachFD, we support fire service candidates at every stage. From building a roadmap for beginners and grads to troubleshooting applications that haven’t been landing, we’ll use virtual sessions to create a focused plan tailored to your goals. Wherever you are in the process, we’ve got your back.

Plan your career path with expert guidance and actionable steps.

Who This Is For

  • New candidates — no training or experience, just exploring what’s required.
  • Recent graduates — wondering how to turn school into next steps.
  • Experienced applicants — applying repeatedly but not moving forward.

How It Works

  • Before the session: You’ll fill out a short form about your background, where you are now, and what you’ve tried.
  • During the session (60 min): We’ll go through your situation step by step. I’ll share real options: schools, certifications, or other resources available in your region.
  • After the session: You’ll receive a written outline of your next steps so you aren’t left guessing.

What You’ll Get

  • A practical roadmap, based on where you are right now.
  • A list of training programs, schools, and courses to explore (with pros and cons).
  • Honest feedback if something is missing from your application.
  • A clearer understanding of how to move forward.

This isn’t a magic key to get hired tomorrow. It’s a plan you can work with. The effort and outcome will depend on you.

FAQ

Q. Do you guarantee I’ll get hired?

A. No. Be extremely weary of anyone who does. What I can guarantee is that you’ll walk away knowing your next steps and where to focus your time.

Q. Is this only for people just starting out?

A. No. It works whether you’re brand new, a recent grad, or already applying without success.

Q. Is this done online?

A. Yes — all sessions are virtual, via Google Meet.
